About Me

My Story

I'm a passionate frontend developer with over 2+ years of experience building web applications that prioritize user experience and accessibility. My journey into tech started with a curiosity about how websites work, which quickly evolved into a deep love for creating digital experiences that make a difference.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring nature with my camera, getting lost in science fiction novels, or strumming my guitar. I believe that diverse experiences outside of work make us better developers and more empathetic problem solvers.

My Goals in This Program

  • Build a portfolio of projects that showcase my skills
  • Learn advanced accessibility patterns and inclusive design
  • Connect with fellow developers and contribute to open source
  • Develop expertise in performance optimization and testing

Areas Where I'm Building Confidence

  • Sharing my knowledge through writing and mentoring others
